ChronoSeal Verification

📜 Instructions:
• Upload a photo of your ID and use the red pen to draw over all personal info except your birthdate.
• Upload a clear selfie for verification.
• Enter your Discord Username (e.g. ChronoUser) and User ID.

🧭 How to find your Discord User ID:
On Desktop:
1. Go to Settings ⚙️ > Advanced
2. Turn on Developer Mode
3. Right-click your username in a server > Copy User ID

On Mobile:
1. Tap your profile picture > Advanced
2. Enable Developer Mode
3. Go to a server, tap your profile, then tap ⋮ > Copy ID

If you're unsure, ask a mod for help!
